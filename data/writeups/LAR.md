## The case for #1

The Rams grade out at the very top of the league — the rare team that pairs an elite quarterback with positive grades on *both* the offensive supporting cast and the defense.

## Quarterback

Matthew Stafford (+5.5) is a top-tier grade, one of the highest QB values in the model, and the foundation of the number one rating.

## What moved the number

- **Offense (+1.0):** Puka Nacua and Kyren Williams headline a genuinely strong group around Stafford.
- **Defense (+1.0):** a major addition — landing Myles Garrett (from Cleveland) plus McDuffie and Watson turns the defense into a real strength.

## Bottom line

No weaknesses on the sheet. Elite QB, plus offense, plus a Garrett-anchored defense — the most complete roster in the model. The one caveat: an older franchise QB carries availability risk that a full season will test.
