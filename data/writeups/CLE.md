## The case for a low rating

Cleveland grades poorly, and it's almost entirely the quarterback. The defense is genuinely good — the offense is the anchor.

## Quarterback

Deshaun Watson (−2.5) is one of the lowest starter grades in the model, and the single biggest weight on this team's number.

## What moved the number

- **Offense (−1.0):** the line was rebuilt (Howard, Z. Johnson, Jenkins), but there's no QB play to reward it.
- **Defense (+1.0):** a real strength even after losing Myles Garrett to the Rams — Verse and Q. Williams keep the front dangerous.

## Bottom line

An above-average defense stuck behind the league's most negative QB situation. The defensive grade is the part of this roster worth taking seriously.
